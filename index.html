About Us

JoJo's started out of our garage in SoCal. Growing up in that area, we were always surrounded by art. Eventually...it grabbed hold of us and never let go. Our passion for turning the old and worn out into something new has taken our company to where we are to today.

Our Heritage

Art is generational in JoJo's family. His grandfather was a carpenter who had a passion for making things look brand new. He taught JoJo to paint and how to work with wood. It's been in the family ever since.
